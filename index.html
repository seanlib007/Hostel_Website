Comforts of your home are as close as you can get at SJU Hostel.

St. Joseph's College Hostel in Bengaluru has magnificently designed hostel for men with all necessary amenities for boarding and other recreational activities. The hostel has its own Canteen to provide healthy and hygienic food to the students. The Canteen is maintained on a co-operative basis.

Architecture

The hostel block building, a semi circular -crescent-shaped stone building, built in 1948 is a remarkable specimen of architecture. The semi-circular building, as distinctive in its architecture as the other College buildings, had a hundred and twenty single rooms and four large halls, one of which is a prayer hall. Two of these halls were later converted into cubicles, thus augmenting the available accommodation to a hundred and eighty. Currently, the hostel can house around 200 students.

Infortainment room & Sports

Spacious common rooms are provided in the hostel for constructive activities like indoor games, discussions and recreation within the hostel. Indoor games like carrom, chess and table tennis - are provided for the inmates. The sports equipment is provided by the sports committee of the hostel. The Hostel has access to a Badminton Court, Basketball courts, Volleyball court, Cricket and Football grounds and facilities for other indoor games.
